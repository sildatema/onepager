About

What is it?

HappyTablr is a reservation system designed to optimize table seating in a new and dynamic way.

Why was it built?

During my internship working with restaurant owners and hospitality businesses, I noticed one complaint that always remained the same: people not showing up for their reservation, leaving tables sitting empty.

The Problem

Current Situation

Reservation systems create a fixed planning schedule from the moment someone makes a reservation. While this works well in many cases, it can lead to empty tables and a lack of flexibility when guests fail to show up or when walk-in customers would like a seat.

❌ Tables Sit Empty

When someone doesn't show up, the table often remains empty. This results in lost revenue and wasted seating capacity.

❌ Complex Workflows

Current reservation systems are usually one of two things: either they are easy to use but lack features, or they offer many features but require extensive onboarding and training.

❌ High Costs

Many systems are expensive, ranging from €50 to €200 per month.

How We Solve It

A simpler approach designed around the actual problem.

One Central Solution

We solve the issues of empty tables, high costs, and extensive onboarding. With an easy-to-use interface and newly designed reservation logic, we minimize empty tables and help maximize profits.

1. What Do You Need to Do?

Purchase our subscription service. It offers low costs, fast setup, and can be ready to use in under an hour.

2. Our Job

We ensure your reservation widget behaves exactly how you want it to and set up the server infrastructure so reservations start arriving instantly.

3. Result

You now have a new and flexible way to ensure guests are seated in the best possible location at any given moment, maximizing table usage.

The Result

Faster

You don't need to spend hours explaining the system to new hires or employees. It's fast, intuitive, and ready to use immediately.

More Reliable

Empty tables become a thing of the past. The system adjusts to real-time changes in reservations and table availability to optimize seating arrangements.

Better Outcomes

More occupied tables lead to higher revenue over time and happier customers who are more likely to return.

How It Works

Step 1

A reservation is created through the widget on your website.

Step 2

It is added to a reservation pool for that date, taking into account the time, date, and number of available tables.

Step 3

When the customer arrives, you simply click a button and the system finds the optimal seating arrangement, maximizing table usage.
